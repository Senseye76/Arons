About me

Originating from Örebro, Sweden, I am a creative visionary who inspires people to explore more, feel more, and think differently through digital storytelling. With a love for nature and adventure, I share moments from my own life, turning experiences into stories. I'm all about meaningful content and purposeful collaborations that inspire people to live a little fuller and get out there.

My approach

I believe the best content comes from real moments and honest emotion. Whether it’s a short video or a full campaign, I focus on telling stories that people actually feel something from. Every project I take on is rooted in purpose, whether it’s to inspire, inform, or simply connect.
